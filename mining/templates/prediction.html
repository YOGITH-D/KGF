Feature Summary

% Iron Feed -

Percentage of iron in the incoming ore feed.


% Silica Feed -

Percentage of silica (impurity) in the incoming ore feed.


Starch Flow -

Amount of starch added (used as a depressant to prevent iron particles from floating).


Amina Flow -

Amount of amine reagent added (collector that helps silica particles attach to bubbles and float).


Ore Pulp Flow -

Volume/flow rate of the ore slurry entering the flotation system.


Ore Pulp pH -

Acidity/alkalinity of the ore slurry, which affects reagent performance.


Ore Pulp Density -

Concentration of solids in the slurry (how thick or diluted the pulp is).


Flotation Column Air Flow (1-7) -

Air injected into each flotation column. controls bubble generation for particle attachment.


Flotation Column Level (1-7) -

Height of slurry/froth in each flotation column. affects froth stability and separation efficiency.
